The Concept

A Small-Footprint, Design-Forward
Property Built Around Zome Structures

Seven Feathers is designed to serve both individual guests and retreat facilitators, combining consistent STR income with higher-yield retreat buyouts and a differentiated, experience-driven stay.

Consistent STR Income

Nightly bookings provide baseline occupancy and steady cash flow across three distinct accommodation units.

Higher-Yield Retreat Buyouts

Full-site rentals for multi-day retreats command premium pricing, served to independent facilitators who need dedicated space.

Experience-Driven Differentiation

Zome architecture, creek access, stargazing, and nature immersion create a guest experience that markets itself.

Healing & Restoration

Grounded in Practice,
Built with Intention

Seven Feathers is not just a place to sleep. It is a place designed to help people return to themselves.

Amy Rohan is a certified Usui Reiki Master with a degree in psychology and more than 25 years of experience working with individuals and families through life's most challenging seasons, including over a decade supporting survivors of domestic violence, trafficking, and sexual abuse.

That work shaped everything about how Seven Feathers is built: trauma-informed care, consent at the center of all healing work, and a deep belief that sovereignty over your own body, spirit, and path is non-negotiable.

Reiki sessions are already offered to cottage guests and will be fully integrated into the retreat experience. Healing circles, forest bathing walks, and stargazing evenings are part of the growing programming, all optional, all grounded in the same principle: you are whole, and this place simply creates the conditions for you to remember that.
