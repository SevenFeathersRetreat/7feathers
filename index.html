The Concept

A Small-Footprint, Design-Forward
Property Built Around Zome Structures

Seven Feathers is designed to serve both individual guests and retreat facilitators, combining consistent STR income with higher-yield retreat buyouts and a differentiated, experience-driven stay.

🏡

Consistent STR Income

Nightly bookings provide baseline occupancy and steady cash flow across three distinct accommodation units.

📅

Higher-Yield Retreat Buyouts

Full-site rentals for multi-day retreats command premium pricing, served to independent facilitators who need dedicated space.

✨

Experience-Driven Differentiation

Zome architecture, creek access, stargazing, and nature immersion create a guest experience that markets itself.

Sacred Geometry, Built to Last

The Structures That
Hold the Space

The guest dwellings at Seven Feathers are Zomes, geometric structures rooted in the zonohedron, one of the oldest forms in sacred geometry. There is something about round spaces that changes how people feel the moment they step inside.

◇

Sacred Geometry

Built on the zonohedron, a form found in nature's own architecture. Round spaces have been used for ceremony, gathering, and rest across cultures for millennia.

🌱

Chemical-Free & Non-Toxic

Bio-ceramic MgO panels create a safe, non-toxic sanctuary. No off-gassing, no volatile compounds, just a clean space for rest and renewal.

🌍

Eco-Friendly Construction

Renewable materials, nearly zero waste during production, no heavy machinery required. Built in 5 to 10 days on a non-permanent foundation, leaving the land beneath untouched.

🛡️

Built to Endure

Fireproof to 2,200°F, waterproof, mold-proof, pest-proof, and rated for 140 MPH winds. These structures protect what matters, quietly and for the long term.
